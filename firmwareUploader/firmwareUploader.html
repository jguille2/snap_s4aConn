S4A Connector requires boards with Firmata firmware uploaded. A lot of devices support Standard Firmata. It is tested on UNO, Nano, Mega, Leonardo, Micro, Due, 101, ESP8266 and NodeMCU.

Uploading Firmata firmwares direcly your UNO compatible board:
  • Be sure you are using Chromium/Chrome/Edge browser and you are under https
  • Plug your UNO by USB
  • Choose your firmware and just upload it!
